Welcome to Pets-R-Us!

Our company started 13 years ago by a group of passionate pet owners who saw a need for quality pet care in our community. We love giving special attention to all our patron's pets as every pet deserves care and attention. Big and small, we'll take care of them all! We are always learning about the latest training methods and testing out the most cutting edge pet products so you can rest assured that our products and services will be the latest and greatest in the pet care world. If you have any specialty needs that we don't currently offer, let us know and we'll do our best to go above and beyond to help you out.
